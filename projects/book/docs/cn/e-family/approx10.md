
## $2 < e < 3$

注意到

$$
\begin{aligned}
L &= \int_0^1 x^2 e^x \, \mathrm{d}x = e-2\\
U &= \int_0^1 x^2 e^x \, \mathrm{d}x = 3-e\\
\end{aligned}
$$

而左边积分项都是正数, 相乘也是正数, 因此有

$$
\begin{aligned}
L &= e-2 > 0\\
U &= 3-e > 0\\
\end{aligned}
$$

即 $2 < e < 3$
